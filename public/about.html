About: Garrett Reynolds

Garrett Reynolds is a profesional BMX athelete, from Toms River, New Jersey.

Garrett is often regarded as the "GOAT" of the sport. Since an early age, hes been on the forefront of innovation and technical street riding.
Even now at the age of 32, in a sport thats not necessarily forgiving on the body, he continues to push the sport leaps and bounds. Inspiring everyone from the old generation to the new.
